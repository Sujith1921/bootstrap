Interior Build Quality

Rolls-Royce interiors are known for their luxury, craftsmanship, and attention to detail: Materials Rolls-Royce uses a variety of materials, including leather, wood, and glass.
Leather: Rolls-Royce uses premium leather upholstery with custom piping and stitching. They also use a technique called tumbling the hides in pigment, which gives the leather an intense color that permeates the entire material.
Wood: Rolls-Royce uses wood like Macassar ebony and Paldao. They also use Malabar teak, which is carved from successive slices of the same tree to maintain a uniform grain, color, and age. Glass: Rolls-Royce uses an uninterrupted glass roof to flood the interior with natural light. Headliners Rolls-Royce uses a Starlight Headliner, which is made of hundreds of fiber-optic lights that create a twinkling starlit sky effect. The brightness of each "star" can be adjusted. Bespoke Audio Rolls-Royce uses a Bespoke Audio system that's tuned by experts to provide studio-quality sound. Bespoke line Rolls-Royce's Bespoke line offers a range of technologies that can be tailored to the customer's preferences.
